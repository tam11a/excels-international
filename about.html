about us

Excels Consultancy is a student consultancy firm that operates globally and offers educational and consultancy services to students who wants to study in the United Kingdom and Canada. As getting admitted into a University can sometimes be adaunting task and with that comes a lot of procedural work, Excels Consultancy will be offering all their services for FREE OF COST, where we will be guiding and assisting the students in respect to their University applications and Visa Applications. Having branches in the United Kingdom, Bangladesh, Malaysia and Pakistan, Excels International aims to provide simplified, professional and efficient services to students. Our firm helps as a benefitting complement for students, as we help them to discover their merits and potential, so they can make an informed andmatured decision on which level of education is best suited to them, which course to select and which University can benefit their future. Operating worldwide, Excels Consultancy ensures that our experienced and well trained consultants provide a competent standard of work and service to students around the globe.

Excels Consultancy has affiliations with over 100 Universities across the UK and around 350 Colleges and Universities in Canada. The quintessential aim of Excels Consultancy is to provide the smoothest and most efficient educational consultancy services to students who are willing to pursue their higher educational opportunities in the UK and Canada, thereby ensuring that all their educational needs are satisfied in an impeccable manner. This additionally provides an increased chance of employment in their career. Acting as abridge and connecting students with their desired Universities, our goal is to provide these students with the best and diligent services and help them achieving their educational goals.
